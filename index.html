Safety, scope & illustration notes

Seek urgent evaluation for new prolonged unresponsiveness, convulsive activity lasting five minutes or more, repeated events without recovery, major injury, breathing difficulty, or a first suspected seizure.

Scope This educational tool does not diagnose seizures or epilepsy.

Illustration Regional boundaries are simplified for education. Internal structures are shown through a conceptual cutaway and are not visible on an intact lateral surface. Clinical validation remains necessary before diagnostic use.
